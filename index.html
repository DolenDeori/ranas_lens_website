Through Rana's Lens photography and video production company specialize in fashion, weddings, concerts & portraits. Our aim is to partner with clients throughout the most meaningful stages of their life and to capture theses moments for reflection for years to come.

About Us

I'm Rana Medhi founder of Through Rana's Lens.

Rana Medhi founded Through Rana's Lens back in 2017 when he was in school in order to a provide premium photography service to artists, models and couples throughout the biggest milestone in their life.
The high quality imagery is paired with a warm and friendly environment that is guaranteed to the best out of each person in front of the camera.Together Through Rana's Lens crew offer a top of the range all-rounded service for every clients who walks through the door.
